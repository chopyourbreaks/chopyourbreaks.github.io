chop your breaks

sure, that amen break you ripped from reddit sounds cool, but if you're going to call it breakcore, your breaks should be broken. chopped. re-arranged.
while you're at it, add some variation and effects- pitch your kicks, roll your snares.

you could even throw in a gabber kick or two...

add a sick filter, distort your fills, and mix multiple breaks together. make it unique.

breakcore as a genre should have chopped breaks.

why? because that's what the genre is about. you could put an ambient pad, chiptune lead, or screaming in your track, but chopped, modified breaks will make it breakcore.
if you don't do that, you don't make breakcore.
